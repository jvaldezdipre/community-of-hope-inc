### Who Is Their Customer?

This is a nonprofit with **three distinct audiences**, each needing different things from the website:

- **Primary Audience:** Women in crisis — experiencing addiction, homelessness, or domestic violence in southeastern CT who need immediate housing and recovery support. Ages 18+, often referred by hospitals, courts, social workers, or treatment centers.
- **Secondary Audience:** Referring professionals — social workers, case managers, hospital discharge planners, treatment center staff, probation officers, pastors, and family members searching on behalf of someone in crisis. These are the people most likely to Google and evaluate the website.
- **Tertiary Audience:** Donors and supporters — individuals, churches, local businesses, and grant-making foundations who want to fund the mission. They need to see impact, credibility, and transparency before giving.
- **Demographics:** Women 18-55 in crisis; referring professionals 25-60; donors skew 35-70, faith-oriented, southeastern CT community members.
- **Service Area:** Southeastern CT — Groton, New London, Norwich, Gales Ferry, Mystic, Stonington, and surrounding communities.
